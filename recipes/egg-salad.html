Egg Salad

Description

This egg salad is a delicious dish that tastes just as great as it looks. It goes well in sandwitches and on rye.

Make deli-worthy egg salad sandwiches at home with this top-rated egg salad recipe! You'll come back to it again and again.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Mayonniase
  • Mustard
  • Green Onion
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Boil the eggs for 7 minutes or until cooked through. Tip: Make a hole on one side of the egg shell to allow faster cooking
  2. Peel and chop the eggs into sizeable amounts.
  3. Add the remaining ingredients and stir for uniform mixture.
